THE 3 RULES THAT WILL DOUBLE YOUR REACH IMMEDIATELY
Rule 1 — Kill the link in the tweet body.
Move every URL to your first reply. This alone removes a 30–50% algorithmic penalty you're currently taking on every single post.
Rule 2 — Reply to big accounts before you post.
Find Indian AI accounts with 10K–100K followers (Ishan Sharma, Varun Mayya, etc.) and leave genuinely smart replies. Their audience sees you. This is faster than any content strategy for building followers from 0.
Rule 3 — Spend 40% time on engagement, 60% on creation.
At under 1K followers, engagement drives growth faster than posting frequency. Replies, quote tweets with takes, and joining conversations matter more than new posts.
